In a remarkable achievement for the gaming industry, PEARL ABYSS has announced that its open-world action-adventure title, Crimson Desert, has surpassed 5 million copies in global sales within just 26 days of its launch. This milestone not only highlights the game's immense popularity but also sets a new record for the fastest sales pace in Korean console game history. 

The company shared this exciting news on April 15, expressing gratitude to the players, affectionately known as Greymanes, who have explored the vast world of Pywel and contributed to the games success. For context, this achievement outpaces the previous record held by Clair Obscur: Expedition 33, which took five months to reach the same sales figure after winning Game of the Year at The Game Awards last year.

As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, it will be fascinating to see how Crimson Desert influences future titles and what innovations PEARL ABYSS will bring next. Could this be the beginning of a new trend in rapid sales growth for blockbuster games?

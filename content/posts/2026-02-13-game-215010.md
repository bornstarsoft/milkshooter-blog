Sony's latest PlayStation State of Play presentation has left fans buzzing with excitement, showcasing over 30 game announcements that promise to redefine the gaming landscape. Additionally, the confirmation of the Metal Gear Solid: Master Collection 2, which includes the long-awaited arrival of Metal Gear Solid 4 on modern consoles, marks a significant moment for nostalgic gamers. With new titles like Castlevania: Belmonts Curse and a John Wick game starring Keanu Reeves, the future of PlayStation looks incredibly promising.

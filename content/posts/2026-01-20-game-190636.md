If youre someone who finds joy in tidiness and aesthetics, then the upcoming game Hozy might just be your next obsession. This delightful sandbox puzzle game combines the satisfying elements of cleaning and decorating, reminiscent of titles like Unpacking and PowerWash Simulator. Players can immerse themselves in a world where they can tidy up rooms and unleash their creativity in arranging furniture, all while enjoying a relaxing gameplay experience.

In Hozy, each level presents a unique room to clean and decorate, allowing players to engage in a fulfilling cycle of tidying up and personalizing spaces. The freedom to place furniture wherever you like, without any strict rules, adds a layer of creativity that many gamers will appreciate. Its not just about cleaning; its about creating a space that reflects your style and preferences.

As we look forward to its release, Hozy promises to be a refreshing addition to the gaming landscape, especially for those who thrive on order and design. What will you create in your perfect virtual space?

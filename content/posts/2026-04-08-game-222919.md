Sony has just unveiled an exciting initiative called The Playerbase, allowing gamers to audition for a chance to be scanned and featured in Gran Turismo 7. This innovative community program not only brings players closer to the games they love but also offers a unique opportunity to leave a lasting mark in the gaming world.

To participate, players can sign in with their PlayStation account on the Playerbase website and share their gaming experiences. After a selection process that includes video interviews, one lucky player will be chosen to appear as an in-game character portrait in Gran Turismo 7. This experience includes a day at a visual arts studio in LA, where the winner will be scanned and even help design a custom vehicle livery.

As Sony looks to expand The Playerbase with more PlayStation titles, this program could redefine how gamers interact with their favorite franchises. Are you ready to take your gaming experience to the next level and potentially see yourself in a game?

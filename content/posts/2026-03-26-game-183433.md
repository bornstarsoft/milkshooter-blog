CRKD has just unveiled the ATOM+, a revolutionary keychain-sized controller that promises to redefine portable gaming. With its compact design and advanced features, this controller is set to deliver full-fledged gaming experiences across a variety of platforms, including the Nintendo Switch 2, PC, and mobile devices. The ATOM+ not only fits easily on your keychain but also boasts cutting-edge technology to eliminate stick drift, ensuring precision gameplay wherever you are.

The ATOM+ is packed with features that gamers crave, such as dual thumbsticks, action buttons, and Bluetooth connectivity for seamless wireless play. Its rechargeable USB-C battery means you can game on the go without worrying about running out of power. Plus, with the CRKD Companion App, players can customize their experience by remapping buttons and adjusting settings, making it a truly personalized gaming tool.

As we look forward to its release in June 2026, the ATOM+ stands as a testament to how innovation can pack powerful capabilities into a small package. Will this be the portable gaming solution weve all been waiting for? Only time will tell.

The highly anticipated digital release of The Super Mario Galaxy Movie is set for next week, with reports indicating that it will be available for purchase on May 5. This release will allow fans to buy and own the film digitally, streaming it on platforms like Apple TV, Prime Video, and YouTube Movies for $24.99. For those who prefer a temporary viewing option, renting the film for $19.99 for a 48-hour period will also be available.

Interestingly, this digital release comes just a month after the film's theatrical debut, which is a quicker turnaround compared to its predecessor, The Super Mario Bros Movie. While Universal Pictures has yet to confirm the date, the source of this information has a strong track record for accuracy. Additionally, fans are eager to know when the film will make its way to subscription streaming services, with the first film having a staggered release across platforms.

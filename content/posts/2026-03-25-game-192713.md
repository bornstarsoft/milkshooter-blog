In an exciting leak for gamers, it has been revealed that the main game for April's PlayStation Plus Essential tier will be none other than *Lords of the Fallen*. This highly anticipated title, a reboot of the 2014 action-RPG, has already made waves since its release in October 2023, selling over a million copies within just ten days. With its dark fantasy setting and engaging gameplay, its poised to be a major draw for subscribers.

*Lords of the Fallen* invites players to step into the shoes of a Dark Crusader on a quest to defeat the demon God, Adyr. With solid reviews and a rich narrative, its clear why this game is being touted as a significant addition to the PS Plus lineup. Available from April 7 to May 5, players will have the chance to claim this title and enjoy it as long as they maintain their subscription.

In a world where game development is often dominated by large studios, the story of Fahim Faysal and his game Compress(space) shines a light on the potential of indie developers from Bangladesh. With a unique approach to puzzle mechanics, the game challenges players to manipulate space itself, offering a fresh perspective on gameplay that encourages experimentation and curiosity.

Fahim's journey from a passionate gamer to a game developer is inspiring, especially considering the challenges faced in a country with limited support for the gaming industry. His perseverance, coupled with the collaboration of a multinational team, showcases how creativity and determination can lead to remarkable outcomes, even in the face of adversity.

As Compress(space) continues to evolve, it stands as a testament to the growing presence of Bangladeshi developers in the global gaming landscape. What other hidden gems might emerge from regions often overlooked by the mainstream gaming industry?

The newly launched visual puzzle game AiliA is captivating gamers with its innovative mirror-based mechanics. Developed by IceToad Studio LLC and published by Gamirror Games, AiliA invites players to step into a world where reality and reflection intertwine, offering a unique gameplay experience that challenges traditional puzzle-solving methods.

In AiliA, players assume the role of Aria, a young girl navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blend the real world with a mirrored dimension. The game features over 100 puzzles that utilize interactive mirrors, allowing players to manipulate their environment and uncover hidden paths. This engaging gameplay is complemented by a special collaboration bundle with Moncage, providing additional value for puzzle enthusiasts.

With a launch price of $11.99 and a 20% discount for early adopters, AiliA is not just a game; it's an invitation to explore the depths of creativity and problem-solving. As players dive into this immersive experience, they are left to ponder: how far can the interplay of light and reflection take us in the realm of gaming?

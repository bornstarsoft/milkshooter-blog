Grasshopper Manufacture, the studio behind cult classics, is set to release another game by the end of 2026, alongside their latest title, *Romeo is a Dead Man*. In a heartfelt video, founder Suda51 shared that both games are dedicated to the late Tatsushi Fujita, a cherished member of the team who passed away during development. This dedication not only honors Fujitas legacy but also highlights the emotional depth that often accompanies game development.

Suda51 also provided updates on the anticipated physical release and art book for *Romeo is a Dead Man*, assuring fans that the studio is actively working on these projects. Furthermore, he mentioned ongoing efforts to port the game to the upcoming Nintendo Switch 2. With so much in the pipeline, fans have plenty to look forward to in the coming months.

Kodansha Game Lab and Octorain have unveiled TrailRail, a captivating 2D action puzzle game that promises to redefine the genre on PC via Steam. The game introduces an innovative mechanic where players can materialize their movement trail with a single button press, allowing for a unique blend of creativity and strategy in solving puzzles. With a free demo available during Steam Next Fest, gamers can dive into this intriguing world and experience its charm firsthand.

In TrailRail, players assume the role of Rail, a girl on a quest to uncover her lost memories while navigating through 38 engaging puzzles. The controls are refreshingly simplelimited to moving, jumping, and materializing trajectoriesyet the challenges require players to think outside the box. Whether transforming a flight path into a bridge or creating footholds with circles, the game emphasizes intuition and exploration, making each stage a rewarding experience.

As you guide Rail through her journey, you'll encounter the enigmatic Bot, a robot companion that adds depth to the narrative. Together, they unravel the mysteries of the world, inviting players to reflect on themes of discovery and self-identity. Will you help Rail uncover her origins and embrace the adventure that awaits?

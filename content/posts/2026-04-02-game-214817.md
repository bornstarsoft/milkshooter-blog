Minos, launching on April 9, redefines strategy puzzle games by placing players in control of the labyrinth itself, a fresh twist on the classic Greek myth of the Minotaur. Instead of merely escaping the maze, players must strategically design it to protect Asterion, the Minotaur, from invading heroes seeking glory.

The game emphasizes labyrinth-building gameplay, allowing players to manipulate walls, gates, and corridors to create intricate paths that confuse and trap their foes. Each run introduces new enemies and traps, ensuring that no two nights are the same, which adds a layer of replayability that is sure to engage both casual and hardcore gamers alike.

As players delve deeper into the game, they will uncover more about the characters of Daedalus and Asterion while feeding the sentient maze with the blood of fallen adventurers. This unique narrative element, combined with the strategic gameplay, positions Minos as a must-play for fans of both mythology and puzzle games. Will you be able to outsmart the heroes and protect the Minotaur?

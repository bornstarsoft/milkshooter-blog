Get ready for an enchanting experience as ININ, alongside Studio Siesta and Rocket Engine, prepares to launch the highly anticipated Trouble Witches FINAL! Episode 01 Daughters of Amalgam. With its unique blend of bullet-hell mechanics and adorable visuals, this game promises to captivate both casual gamers and hardcore fans alike.

The Special Edition, available for pre-order now, offers a treasure trove of goodies, including a physical copy of the game, an original soundtrack, a full-color artbook, and a digital download code for exclusive DLC. Originally released in 2007, Trouble Witches has undergone significant evolution, and this latest installment showcases stunning hand-drawn designs and a revamped combat system that enhances the overall gameplay experience.

Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the genre, Trouble Witches FINAL! Episode 01 Daughters of Amalgam is poised to deliver an unforgettable Cute 'Em Up adventure. Will you join the ranks of these adorable witches and dive into the magical chaos?

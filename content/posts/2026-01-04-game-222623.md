The recent release of iRacing's NASCAR 25 Console game marks a significant milestone for fans of NASCAR gaming, particularly with its impressive array of base paint schemes. This edition not only showcases a variety of drivers but also introduces the ARCA Menards Series to the console gaming realm, enriching the overall experience for players and fans alike. While some notable sponsors like Monster Energy and alcoholic beverage brands are absent, the game still delivers a nostalgic trip down memory lane with the return of iconic sponsors such as Red Bull, making its first appearance in a NASCAR game since 2011.

Despite the exclusions, the sheer volume of paint schemes available is a testament to the developers' commitment to providing a diverse and engaging gaming experience. Fans can revel in the vibrant designs and unique branding that have become synonymous with NASCAR culture.

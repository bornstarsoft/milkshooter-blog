Battlefield 6 has made history by clinching the coveted Game of the Year award at the UKIE Video Game Awards 2026, alongside accolades for Best UK PC Game. This remarkable achievement not only highlights the game's exceptional quality but also its massive commercial success, with over seven million copies sold within just three days of its release.

Developed by Battlefield Studios with contributions from Criterion Games, Battlefield 6 has set a new benchmark for the series, becoming the top-selling game in the American market for 2025. The awards ceremony also recognized other notable titles, including Atomfall as the Best UK Console Game and PowerWash Simulator as the Best UK Mobile Game, showcasing the diverse landscape of gaming excellence.

The excitement is building for gamers as BeXide announces the release of the multiplayer party game, Fruit Mountain Party, on Switch 2 and Switch in spring 2026. Currently in Early Access for PC, this vibrant 3D puzzle game has already captured the attention of players with its unique fruit-combining mechanics and engaging multiplayer modes. The upcoming console versions promise to expand the fun, allowing players to join in on the action both online and locally.

Fruit Mountain Party will make its debut at major gaming events, including Osaka Game Dungeon and Tokyo eSports Festa 2026, giving attendees a first look at the Switch versions. With features like heated online battles and a competitive single-player mode, the game is designed to keep players engaged and challenged. Whether you're stacking fruits for high scores or battling friends, the game offers a fresh take on party gaming that is sure to delight.

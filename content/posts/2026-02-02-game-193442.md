PlayStation Plus subscribers have a unique opportunity to dive into a chilling yet captivating experience with The Exit 8, a free puzzle game that has garnered attention for its eerie atmosphere and engaging gameplay. This indie gem, developed by Kotake Create, invites players to navigate a hauntingly repetitive journey through a Japanese metro tunnel, where the goal is to identify unsettling anomalies that disrupt the environment.

What sets The Exit 8 apart is its blend of horror and puzzle-solving elements. While not strictly a horror game, the unsettling visuals and the need to remain vigilant against creepy anomalies create a tense atmosphere that keeps players on edge. With a completion time of just a couple of hours, its an ideal pick for those looking for a quick yet memorable gaming experience.

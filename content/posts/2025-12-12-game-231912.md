The highly anticipated PlayStation 5 exclusive, Saros, has officially been delayed until April 20, 2026. Originally set for a March release, this decision comes as a disappointment to many eager fans. However, the delay may provide the developers at the Returnal studio with additional time to refine the game, ensuring a polished experience upon launch.

Saros promises to deliver an emotional and powerful narrative, set on the hostile alien planet of Carcosa. Players will step into the shoes of Arjun Devraj, a Soltari Enforcer, as they navigate a world that shifts with every death. The games unique mechanics, including a mix of human and alien weaponry, are designed to enhance the gameplay experience, making each run feel fresh and engaging.

With a Digital Deluxe Edition available for pre-order, players can look forward to 48-hour early access, giving them a chance to dive into the haunting story ahead of others.

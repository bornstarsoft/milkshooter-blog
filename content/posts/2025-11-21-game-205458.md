The 2025 Golden Joystick Awards showcased the best in gaming, with Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 leading the pack by winning a remarkable seven awards, including the coveted Ultimate Game of the Year. This breakout title from Sandfall Interactive not only captivated players with its stunning visual design and compelling storytelling but also highlighted the exceptional performances of its lead and supporting actors, Jennifer English and Ben Starr, who took home individual accolades for their roles.

In a year where public voting played a significant role in determining the winners, Clair Obscur's dominance is a testament to its widespread appeal among gamers. Other notable winners included Ghost of Ytei, which clinched awards for Best Audio Design and Console Game of the Year, and Hollow Knight: Silksong, recognized as Best Indie Game and PC Game of the Year. As the gaming community reflects on these achievements, one can't help but wonder what innovations and experiences the upcoming titles will bring to the table.

Moon Active has just launched Jelly Busters: Puzzle Game, a vibrant arcade-style puzzler that invites players into a colorful skatepark filled with elastic jellies. This game uniquely combines the thrill of shooting colorful jelly shots with the satisfaction of uncovering hidden images beneath stacks of glowing cubes. Each shot not only contributes to gameplay but also reveals a small piece of art, making every level a delightful experience.

As players progress, they encounter increasingly complex challenges, including tougher cube arrangements and unlockable boosters that enhance gameplay. The soothing ASMR-style feedback from popping cubes and bouncing jellies adds to the immersive experience, making it both relaxing and engaging. With leaderboards tracking global performance, Jelly Busters encourages friendly competition, turning each cleared board into a measurable achievement.

As mobile gaming continues to evolve, titles like Jelly Busters showcase the potential for innovative gameplay mechanics that blend relaxation with challenge. Will this game set a new standard for puzzle games in the mobile arena? Stay tuned to find out!

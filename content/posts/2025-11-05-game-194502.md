Microsoft has just unveiled an exciting lineup for Xbox Game Pass this November, featuring 11 new titles, including the highly anticipated Call of Duty: Black Ops 7. This addition is sure to thrill gamers, as it will be available to both Game Pass Ultimate and PC Game Pass subscribers starting November 14. The diverse selection of games caters to a wide range of tastes, ensuring that theres something for everyone.

Among the notable titles arriving in the first half of November are Dead Static Drive, Sniper Elite Resistance, and Lara Croft and the Temple of Osiris. With such a robust lineup, players can look forward to new adventures and challenges. However, its essential to note that six games will be leaving the service on November 15, including popular titles like Football Manager 24 and STALKER 2: Heart of Chernobyl.

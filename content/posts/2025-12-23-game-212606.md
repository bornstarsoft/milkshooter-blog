He hints at innovative gameplay that leverages the unique capabilities of Nintendo hardware, suggesting a fresh take on the horror genre that players have yet to experience.

While details remain scarce, Babieno's enthusiasm is palpable. The studio's commitment to crafting a standout experience reflects Babieno's long-standing passion for Nintendo, which he describes as a dream come true.
